CARBOHYDRATES NUTRIENTS

- Food consisting of or containing a lot of sugars, starch, cellulose, or similar substances that can be broken down to release energy in the human body, and make up one of the main nutritional food groups

BENEFITS:

  • Carbohydrates are mainly found in plant foods.

  • Carbohydrates play several roles in living organisms, including providing energy.

  • This article looks at types of carbohydrates, nutrition, and their effects on health.

  • They also occur in dairy products in the form of a milk sugar called lactose.

  • Foods high in carbohydrates include bread, pasta, beans, potatoes, rice, and cereals.

  • We also look at the relationship between carbohydrates and diabetes.
